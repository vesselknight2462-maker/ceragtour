==========================================
  TROUBLESHOOTING
==========================================

Problem: "Authorization required" error
Solution: Redeploy and authorize again

Problem: "Script function not found"
Solution: Make sure you deployed as "Web app", not "API Executable"

Problem: Changes not showing on index.html
Solution: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) to clear browser cache

Problem: "API_URL not configured" alert
Solution: Update API_URL in admin-portal.html with your deployed Web App URL
